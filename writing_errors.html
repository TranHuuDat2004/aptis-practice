Các lỗi sai thường gặp trong Writing

Phân tích và khắc phục các lỗi ngữ pháp cơ bản

Phân tích bài viết mẫu

Dưới đây là một bài viết mẫu và các lỗi đã được sửa lại. Hãy đọc kỹ để hiểu rõ các lỗi sai trong ngữ cảnh thực tế.

Bản gốc (có lỗi):

Dear Manager,
I hope this email finds you well.
My name is Huu Dat Tran and I have been an active member of the English club last year. I am writing to express my thoughts regarding your recent email.
According to the email, you mentioned that many new words and phrases are being added to the language. While some people accept this happily, other react strongly and hope it should be control these changes.
I think language naturally changes because we need new definition such as technology, software and application. Many people like this because it helps they remember this better.
However, some people worry about the language can be become confusing. This is true for the beginner to learn such as children and people learning English as a second language.
Personally, language should develop but also keep it clear to easy understanding. This is why people react negative to this.
Thank you for reading my views. I look forward to your reply.
Best Regards,
Huu Dat Tran

Bản đã sửa (gợi ý):

Dear Manager,
I hope this email finds you well.
My name is Huu Dat Tran and I have been an active member of the English club since last year. I am writing to express my thoughts regarding your recent email.
According to the email, you mentioned that many new words and phrases are being added to the language. While some people accept this happily, others react strongly and hope these changes can be controlled.
I think language naturally changes because we need new definitions such as technology, software and applications. Many people like this because it helps them remember this better.
However, some people worry that the language can become confusing. This is true for beginners such as children and people learning English as a second language.
Personally, language should develop but also stay clear for easy understanding. This is why people react negatively to this.
Thank you for reading my views. I look forward to your reply.
Best Regards,
Huu Dat Tran

Tổng hợp các lỗi sai cơ bản

1. Thiếu động từ "to be"

Sai: I very happy. / I very excited.
Đúng: I am very happy. / I am very excited.
(Quy tắc: Chủ ngữ + to be + tính từ)

2. Sai giới từ

Sai: people on the world / talk other people / chat my friend
Đúng: people around the world / talk to other people / chat with my friend

3. Dùng sai từ loại

Sai: very like / react negative
Đúng: really like (hoặc like... very much) / react negatively
(Quy tắc: Động từ thường đi với trạng từ)

4. Lỗi số ít/số nhiều

Sai: many friend / new thing / other react
Đúng: many friends / new things / others react

5. Thiếu mạo từ (a/an/the)

Sai: added to language / under teacher instruction
Đúng: added to the language / under the teacher’s instruction

6. Sai cấu trúc câu

Sai: language can be become confusing / too hard understand
Đúng: language can become confusing / too hard to understand

7. Sai đại từ tân ngữ

Sai: it helps they remember
Đúng: it helps them remember

8. Sai thì

Sai: have been an active member... last year
Đúng: was an active member... last year (hoặc have been... since last year)

9. Dùng sai từ nối

Sai: Beside that / Honest, I very excited
Đúng: Besides that / Honestly, I am very excited
